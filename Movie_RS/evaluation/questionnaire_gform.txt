GOOGLE FORM — ready-to-paste items
===================================

Paste each block into Google Forms (or Qualtrics) one-by-one.
Use "Linear scale" (1–5) for Q1–Q8 and "Long answer" for Q9–Q10.

---

FORM TITLE
Movie Recommender — A/B Evaluation (COMP4135)

FORM DESCRIPTION
Thanks for helping us evaluate our group-project recommender system.
This questionnaire takes about 3 minutes per condition. All answers
are anonymous. Please fill it in twice — once after you have used
condition A (Original) and once after condition B (Enhanced).

---

SECTION 1 — identification
(use "Short answer" type, mark as required)

1. Participant ID (given by the team, e.g. P01)
2. Condition you just used            [multiple choice: A (Original) / B (Enhanced)]
3. Was this your first or second try? [multiple choice: First / Second]

---

SECTION 2 — Likert (1 = strongly disagree, 5 = strongly agree)
(use "Linear scale" 1–5 for every item)

Q1. The recommended movies match my taste.
Q2. The list of recommendations is diverse enough.
Q3. I discovered movies I had not seen or considered before.
Q4. I understood why each movie was recommended to me.
Q5. I felt in control of the recommendations (via feedback / diversity slider).
Q6. The interface was easy to use.
Q7. Overall, I am satisfied with the recommendations.
Q8. I trust the system's recommendations.

---

SECTION 3 — Open questions
(use "Paragraph" type, not required)

Q9. What did you like most about this version?
Q10. What would you change or improve?

---

EXPORTING TO results.csv
========================

After collecting responses, File → Download → CSV. The Google Form
CSV will look something like:

    Timestamp, Participant ID, Condition, ..., Q1, Q2, ...

Rename / reorder the columns to match `results_template.csv`:

    participant_id,order,condition,Q1,Q2,Q3,Q4,Q5,Q6,Q7,Q8,comment_like,comment_improve

- `order` values: "AB" if the participant had A first, "BA" otherwise.
- `condition` values: `A` or `B`.

Then save as `evaluation/results.csv` and run:

    python evaluation/analyze_results.py evaluation/results.csv \
        --out evaluation/results_summary.csv \
        --figures-dir report/figures
